About MSU-MCEST

Your First Choice for Quality Education

With education programs consistently achieving a 100% passing rate in the Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET) and producing board topnotchers, MSU-MCEST continues to aim for excellence in instruction, research, and community service.

The campus envisions further expansion into science, technology, and innovation — responding proactively to the educational and developmental needs of its communities.
